Horse Riding in Trujillo, Peru

This photo was taken in Trujillo, Peru. The city where my dad was raised. Back in the colonial days of Spain's occupation, Trujillo was well known to breed the horses with the smoothest ride in the world. They have kept up with this tradition and they still breed those horses today. Here you can see me very pleased about being able to ride one.
